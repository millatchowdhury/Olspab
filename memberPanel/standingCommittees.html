Seeking EoI for Standing Committees of BASIS (2024-2026)

Dear Industry Colleague,


Greetings from BASIS!


As you know that the newly elected BASIS Executive Council (2024-26) and started functioning. Given the current landscape of BASIS member organizations and the overall ICT sector, we are in the process of forming standing committees to chart and implement strategic initiatives. These standing committees play a vital role in developing policies, action plans, and their execution to foster a business-friendly environment for our member organizations.


We believe that your talent, knowledge, experience, and invaluable contributions would greatly benefit these committees. Therefore, we invite you to express your interest by joining either in a standing committee or a forum where you can share your expertise and actively participate to contribute the best. Members of these committees and forums are expected to attend at least one meeting in every two months.


In this regard, interested companies are requested to submit EoI by 5 PM on W, June 12, 2024 by following the given steps:

  • Log in to your membership profile using this link: https://basis.org.bd/member-login
  • Click on the 'Register Now' button located at the top-right corner under the ‘EoI for BASIS Standing Committee and Forum’ section
  • Fill out the EoI questionnaire
  • Click  'Submit' to complete the EoI process

Please note that, the deadline will not be extended as the member of the committee/forum will be kept limited to make it more effective. 


We look forward to your timely response and your continued support in advancing the ICT sector.

 

Best regards,


Russell T. Ahmed
President, BASISa
