The Software and IT-enabled Services (ITES) industry of Bangladesh is emerging as one of the key contributors to the growth of Bangladesh Economy. The need for efficiency enhancement in the Government and Private sector is growing day to day, which can only be achieved through proper implementation of Software and IT-enabled services. The local IT/ITES companies have proved that they have the necessary capability to fulfill these demands of software and services. Bangladesh Association of Software & Information Services (BASIS), the leading national trade association for this sector, has been serving the interests of the Software and ITES industry of the country since its inception. The achievement of BASIS can be understood by the fact that the association started its journey with only 18 charter members in 1998, and recently, the membership count of BASIS has surpassed 2,400+. Today, BASIS member companies occupy a major share of the revenues generated by the Software and ITES industry. BASIS membership also covers a vast portion of small and medium companies that work with the association for guidance and continuous support. According to the latest Government Gazette circulated on 02 September 2020, membership of BASIS is mandatory for any software/ITES company for doing business in Bangladesh and to participate in any government/non-government procurement. BASIS’s aims and objectives include the Trade Facilitation and business promotion in software and IT enabled services industry; encouraging the advancement of research; facilitation of education, employment, and growth of the Bangladesh economy. In line with these goals, BASIS works with the Government of Bangladesh to formulate policies and procedures in the software and ITES sector and offer a portfolio of services to the software & ITES community of Bangladesh. BASIS has been working in close cooperation with the Bangladesh Government to create a Software and ITES industry-friendly environment. Improving telecom infrastructure, software industry-friendly tax regime, and prompt redress of procedural bottlenecks are signs of the work that BASIS has been undertaking with the government.

Apart from the policy advocacy advantages, the members get exclusive services from BASIS. These services include: Various trade, business operation and investment-related supports to members on the issues with Government agencies. Exclusive participation at events, as such, seminars, webinars, conferences, award giving ceremonies, business matchmaking sessions with the global and local partners, as well as training programs designed for BASIS members only. Discounted facility on the services fee of every event or program compared to the services provided to non-members. Moreover, BASIS is working to make its members including other Bangladeshi ICT firms to get preferential treatment in the Government projects. BASIS is providing cash incentive certificates to Software and ITES exporting companies, against which, the exporting companies may claim and receive 10% cash incentives to be provided by the government on the export value of the company.

Any firm or company thereof registered or incorporated in Bangladesh and conducting business in Software and IT Enabled Services is eligible to apply for membership provided: Its main activity is the development of Software and ITES; It performs this activity for commercial purposes Applicant Company requires submitting work order or business experience for General Membership.

Associate Membership: Those who have operational experience from 0 months to 2 years. In case of a new company/start-up, less than 2 (two) years of age is encouraged to apply for Associate Category Membership. General Membership: Those who have operational experience of more than 2 years. The Company which has more than 4 (four) years of age with three work orders is encouraged to apply for General Category Membership. Organizations that have been established long ago, but have just started an IT business, will be able to apply for General Membership. Affiliate Membership: As per the SRO of NBR, the following nature of businesses are considered as ITES: (Information Technology Enabled Services): Digital Content Development and Management, Animation (Both 2D and 3D), Geographic Information Services (GIS), IT Support and Software Maintenance Services, Website Hosting, Digital Data Analytics, Business Process Outsourcing, Data Entry, Data Processing, Call Centre, Digital Graphics Design and Computer-Aided Design, Search Engine Optimization, Web Document Conversion, Imaging Listing, and Archiving, Overseas Medical Transcription, Cyber Security Services, E-Procurement and E-Auction, Cloud Service, System Integration, e-learning platform, e-book publication, Mobile Application Development, IT Freelancing. Those who are dealing with business other than Software & ITES would be considered as Affiliate members.
